INTRODUCTION

The purpose of this document is to review the state of QAQC for the FGS surveys.

State of DO13C data

Results flags

All of the NA flags for end of 2019 and beginning of 2020 are related to results that have not yet been QCd, therefore, they had no flags attached before being exported to R.

Quality levels

Raw quality levels are from recent FGS surveys that have not been QCd by the oceanography team yet.

2015 missing values were given a Error as Quality level. They have been changed to PI or technician, depending on the situation.
